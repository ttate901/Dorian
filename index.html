About

From Memphis Streets to Kingdom Beats

Whitehaven, Memphis, TN

Born and raised in the Whitehaven area of Memphis, Tennessee, Dorian grew up loving rap music and trap beats that shaped his musical world.

He had natural talent for writing lyrics and understanding rap music, and it seemed like he would make secular music. Everything changed when Dorian gave his life to Jesus. In that life-changing moment, he thought he had to give up his musical skills and the trap beats that had always inspired him. He felt like his old life, including his rap talents, needed to be left behind.

But God had different plans. Dorian's wife encouraged him and helped him see that his musical skills weren't something to give up, but gifts to use for serving God. Her support started a new creative journey that would shape his music.
